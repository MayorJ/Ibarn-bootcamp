About Ibarn Concepts

Ibarn Concepts is a leading software development company with expertise in EdTech, logistics, and agriculture. With over five years of experience, we have successfully trained individuals and businesses across Nigeria, both physically and online. Our mission is to drive technological advancement through education and innovation.

Stemming from the desire to get young people off the street in 2020, we began free software development trainings in Ogun state. Our vision is to ensure an average young Nigerian(African) is able to use technology to drive change within the fast rising global shift to technology and science.

We specialize in custom software solutions, training programs, and consulting services. Our expertise extends to developing digital tools for the logistics and agriculture industries, improving efficiency and productivity through technology.

Software Development Bootcamp

Join our intensive 3-day bootcamp designed for secondary school students. This bootcamp provides hands-on learning in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript/Python, preparing students for the digital economy.

Bootcamp Curriculum

  • Day 1: Introduction to Web Development, HTML Basics, Structuring a Webpage.
  • Day 2: Styling with CSS, Responsive Design, Basic JavaScript Concepts.
  • Day 3: Interactive Web Pages with JavaScript/Python, Final Project & Presentation.
